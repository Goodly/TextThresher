Two arrested at Occupy Sebastopol camp
<Camp>Occupy Sebastopol protests at the southwest corner of Highway 12 and Highway 116, />
<Camp>Police arrested two men with outstanding warrants Sunday at the Occupy Sebastopol encampment on the city's downtown plaza./>
<Camp>Both men are transients in the Sebastopol area and were visiting the tent camp, Officer David Ginn said./>
Jody Raymond Sunde, 38, was arrested on two warrants for violating probation in a drunken driving case and failure to appear in court in a drug case, Ginn said. He was also arrested on suspicion of drinking alcohol, which is a violation of his probation, Ginn said.
James Edward Fenlaw, 55, was also arrested on two warrants, both for failure to appear in court on two separate theft cases, Ginn said.
