LAPD officers shoot man in downtown LA
Sunday, November 13, 2011
KABC-TV covers Los Angeles and Southern California. (KABC Photo)


DOWNTOWN LOS ANGELES (KABC) -- Los Angeles <Camp>police officers shot a man/> in downtown Los Angeles on Saturday.
The suspect was taken to the hospital in critical condition. No officers were injured.
<Camp>Two officers assigned to the Occupy L.A. camp responded to a call about a man with a knife at 7th and Los Angeles streets about 4:45 p.m./> It's unclear what prompted the shooting.
Several streets were closed as the officer-involved shooting was investigated.
It's unknown if a knife was recovered.
