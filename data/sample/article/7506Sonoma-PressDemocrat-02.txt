'Occupy Sonoma' plaza event today
<Info>Activists are planning to gather in Sonoma's Plaza this evening/> to show solidarity with nationwide protests aimed at the country's financial institutions.
“Occupy Sonoma” is being billed as a symbolic rally and march for economic and social justice in the United States.
Protesters have circulated a list of demands, which include a foreclosure moratorium, a financial transaction tax, an end to Bush-era tax cuts, prosecuting financial malfeasance, restoring public sector jobs and a new national jobs program.
<Info>The event, which starts at 5 p.m., is sponsored by Sonoma MoveOn, the Sonoma Peace and Justice Center and Sonoma Valley Democrats./>
