Richmond protesters refuse to remove tents

LENGTH: 130 words
DATELINE: RICHMOND Va.

<Camp>About 25 tents were in place Wednesday at Kanawha Plaza./> Protesters with the Occupy Richmond movement, an offshoot of the economy-driven Occupy Wall Street protest in New York, began their demonstration Saturday.
