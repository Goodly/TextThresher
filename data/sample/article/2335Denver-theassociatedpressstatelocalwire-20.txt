Occupy protesters demonstrate at Colo. Walmart hub
SECTION: STATE AND REGIONAL
LENGTH: 94 words
DATELINE: LOVELAND Colo.


<Protester>Anti-Wall Street protesters are demonstrating outside a Walmart distribution center in northern Colorado./>
<Protester>About 30 people were at the site in Loveland on Monday./>
<Protester>The Loveland Reporter-Herald ( http://bit.ly/vyRvni) reported that one person was arrested for obstructing a roadway./>
<Protester>The protesters are from occupy movements in Denver, Fort Collins and Greeley./>
<Protester>The demonstration is intended to show support for protesters along the West Coast trying to disrupt some of the nation's busiest ports./>
