Jesse Jackson stop in Des Moines is canceled, Occupy group says         
Jens Manuel Krogstad                                         
10:27 AM, Dec 29, 2011
Categories: Caucus Insider                


<Camp>The Rev. Jesse Jackson will not be speaking tonight in Des Moines at the headquarters of the Occupy Caucus group./>
The evening visit by the longtime civil rights activist had been announced Wednesday by demonstrator Ed Fallon and others,<Camp> but organizers said this morning that it was canceled./>
The reason for the switch wasn’t immediately clear.
